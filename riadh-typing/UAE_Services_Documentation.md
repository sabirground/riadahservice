## 2. Visa Services

### New Residency Visa
**Description:** Complete processing for obtaining a new UAE residency visa for individuals sponsored by employers or family members.

**Processing Time:** 3-5 business days (subject to government approval)

**Required Documents:**
- Passport copy (valid 6+ months)
- Passport size photo (white background)
- Emirates ID (if available)
- Entry permit / change status
- Medical test result
- Emirates ID application form
- Trade license (for employment visas)

### Renew Residency Visa
**Description:** Renewal of existing UAE residency visa for individuals and families.

**Processing Time:** 2-4 business days

**Required Documents:**
- Passport copy
- Current residency visa copy
- Tenancy contract (Ejari)
- Medical test result
- Emirates ID
- Labor contract
- Tenancy contract (Ejari)

### Cancel Residency Visa
**Description:** Formal cancellation process for UAE residency visas.

**Processing Time:** 1-2 business days

**Required Documents:**
- Passport copy
- Original Emirates ID
- Visa cancellation form

### Golden Visa Nomination
**Description:** Assistance with the nomination process for UAE Golden Visa eligibility.

**Processing Time:** 10-15 business days

**Required Documents:**
- Passport copy
- Current residency visa
- CV / Resume
- Qualification certificates
- Bank statement / income proof (if required)
- Qualification / experience documents (if applicable)

### Golden Visa Full Process
**Description:** Complete end-to-end processing for obtaining UAE Golden Visa.

**Processing Time:** 15-25 business days

**Required Documents:**
- Passport copy
- Current visa or status
- CV / Resume
- Qualification certificates
- Tenancy contract
- Qualification / tax / salary documents (as per category)

### Transfer Visa To New Passport
**Description:** Transferring existing UAE residency visa to a new passport.

**Processing Time:** 2-3 business days

**Required Documents:**
- New passport copy
- Old passport copy
- Current visa copy
- Emirates ID

### Data Modification
**Description:** Correction or modification of personal data in UAE residency visa or government records.

**Processing Time:** 1-3 business days

**Required Documents:**
- Passport copy
- Current visa copy
- Emirates ID
- Correction request details

### New Born Visa
**Description:** Processing residency visa for newborn babies in the UAE.

**Processing Time:** 3-5 business days

**Required Documents:**
- Birth certificate
- Parents passport copies
- Parents visa copies
- Hospital birth notification

### Dependents On Hold
**Description:** Resolution for dependents' visa status issues and reinstatement.

**Processing Time:** 2-4 business days

**Required Documents:**
- Passport copy
- Current visa copy
- Sponsor documents
